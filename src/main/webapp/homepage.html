How we can help

Learn

     Schedule face-to-face tutoring sessions with real people to get your questions answered effectively. Make sure to use our progress tracker feature to input your past experiences and future goals to help your tutors tailor their lessons to your needs. Additionally, explore or create study groups to post questions asynchronously and to connect to other students.

Read

     Explore our books search feature to find reading materials to complement your study. Moreoever, follow curated book recommendation lists made by tutors who are experts in their field.

Share

     Contribute to our community by signing up as a tutor and helping others learn topics you are comfortable with. Begin by selecting the topics you would like to tutor in, add available time slots, and view your students's progress and goals under your "my students" page. If you are a student who feels comfortable with what you have learned, make sure to sign up as a tutor for those topics and pass down the knowledge you have learned!
